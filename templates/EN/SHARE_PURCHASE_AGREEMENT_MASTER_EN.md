# Share Purchase Agreement

This Share Purchase Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "Effective Date").

## 1. Parties

**Seller**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF SELLER]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY/INDIVIDUAL]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR No. / CPR No.: **[IDENTIFICATION NUMBER]**

**Buyer**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF BUYER]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY/INDIVIDUAL]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR No. / CPR No.: **[IDENTIFICATION NUMBER]**

The Seller and the Buyer are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Background

### 2.1 Ownership

The Seller is the legal and beneficial owner of the shares described in Section 3.

### 2.2 Intent

The Buyer wishes to purchase such shares on the terms set out in this Agreement.

## 3. Sale of Shares

### 3.1 Sale

The Seller agrees to sell and transfer to the Buyer **[NUMBER]** shares in **[COMPANY NAME]**, representing **[X%]** of the share capital (the "Shares").

### 3.2 Title

The Shares are sold free from any liens, encumbrances, or third-party rights unless disclosed in Schedule 1.

## 4. Purchase Price

### 4.1 Price

The total purchase price for the Shares is **[AMOUNT]** **[CURRENCY]** (the "Purchase Price").

### 4.2 Payment

The Purchase Price shall be paid:
- in full on the Closing Date, or
- in accordance with Schedule 2 (Installments / Escrow / Earn-Out)

### 4.3 Payment Method

Payment shall be made to the Seller’s designated bank account.

## 5. Closing

### 5.1 Closing Date

Closing shall take place on **[CLOSING DATE]** (the "Closing Date").

### 5.2 Deliveries

On the Closing Date:
- the Seller shall deliver signed share transfer documents
- the Buyer shall pay the Purchase Price
- the Company shall update its shareholder register

## 6. Representations and Warranties of the Seller

The Seller represents and warrants that:
- the Seller has full legal authority to sell the Shares
- the Shares are fully paid and free from encumbrances
- to the Seller’s knowledge, the Company is duly incorporated and validly existing
- to the Seller’s knowledge, the Company is not subject to undisclosed litigation
- to the Seller’s knowledge, the Company has complied with applicable laws
- to the Seller’s knowledge, the Company has disclosed all material liabilities

## 7. Representations and Warranties of the Buyer

The Buyer represents and warrants that:
- the Buyer has authority to enter into this Agreement
- the Buyer has sufficient funds to pay the Purchase Price

## 8. Indemnification

### 8.1 Seller Indemnity

The Seller shall indemnify the Buyer for losses arising from breach of Seller’s representations and warranties.

### 8.2 Claims Period

Claims must be notified within **[X months]** after Closing.

### 8.3 Liability Cap

The Seller’s total liability shall not exceed the Purchase Price, except in cases of fraud or wilful misconduct.

## 9. Covenants

### 9.1 Ordinary Course

From signing until Closing, the Seller shall ensure that the Company conducts business in the ordinary course.

### 9.2 Restrictions

No material changes shall be made without the Buyer’s consent.

## 10. Confidentiality

The Parties shall keep confidential all non-public information relating to this transaction.

## 11. Non-Competition (Optional)

The Seller agrees not to compete with the Company for a period of **[X months/years]** within **[Territory]**, subject to applicable law.

## 12. Taxes and Costs

### 12.1 Costs

Each Party shall bear its own costs related to this transaction.

### 12.2 Transfer Taxes

Any transfer taxes shall be borne by **[Buyer/Seller]**, unless otherwise required by law.

## 13. Termination

### 13.1 Pre-Closing Termination

This Agreement may be terminated prior to Closing by mutual written consent.

### 13.2 Effects

If terminated before Closing, neither Party shall have further obligations except confidentiality.

## 14. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[CITY, DENMARK]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 15. Severability

If any provision is invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.

## 16. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the sale of the Shares. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 17. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
