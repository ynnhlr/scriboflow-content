### 6.1 Management Rights

Each Partner has equal rights in management unless otherwise agreed.

The following decisions require unanimous approval:
- admission of new Partners
- incurrence of debt exceeding **[Number] [Currency]**
- sale of material assets
- dissolution of the Partnership

Day-to-day operations may be delegated as agreed.

## 7. Authority

Each Partner may bind the Partnership in matters within ordinary course of business unless restricted in writing.

## 8. Duties and Conduct

### 8.1 Loyalty

Partners shall act loyally and in the best interests of the Partnership.

### 8.2 Non-Compete

Partners shall not engage in competing activities without consent of the other Partners.

## 9. Banking and Accounting

### 9.1 Bank Account

Partnership funds shall be held in a dedicated bank account.

### 9.2 Records

Proper accounting records shall be maintained.

### 9.3 Financial Year

## 10. Admission of New Partners

## 11. Withdrawal

### 11.1 Notice

### 11.2 Buyout

The remaining Partners may purchase the withdrawing Partner’s interest at fair market value determined by:
- **[Valuation method]**, or
- **[Valuation method]**

## 12. Death or Incapacity

In case of death or permanent incapacity of a Partner:
- the remaining Partners may purchase the affected Partner’s interest
- valuation shall follow Section 11

## 13. Dissolution

### 13.1 Dissolution

The Partnership may be dissolved by unanimous agreement.

### 13.2 Winding Up

Upon dissolution:
- assets shall be liquidated
- liabilities paid
- remaining proceeds distributed according to ownership percentages

## 14. Liability

Unless otherwise structured under applicable law, the Partners are jointly and severally liable for Partnership obligations.

## 15. Confidentiality

Partners shall keep confidential all business information relating to the Partnership.

## 16. Dispute Resolution

### 16.1 Negotiation

Partners shall first attempt to resolve disputes through negotiation.

### 16.2 Mediation

## 18. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Partners concerning the Partnership. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by all Partners.
