**Restricted Party**
- Full legal name: **[Company name]**
- Address: **[Address]**

The Company and the Restricted Party are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Purpose

### 2.1 Legitimate Interests

The Restricted Party has access to confidential information, trade secrets, customer relationships, and strategic business knowledge.

### 2.2 Objective

This Agreement is intended to protect the Company’s legitimate business interests following termination of employment or contractual engagement.

## 3. Non-Compete Obligation

### 3.1 Restricted Activities

During the Restricted Period, the Restricted Party shall not, directly or indirectly:
- engage in activities that compete with the Company’s core business
- own, manage, control, or be employed by a competing business
- assist or advise any competing entity in a role similar to that performed for the Company

### 3.2 Passive Ownership

Passive ownership of publicly traded shares not exceeding **[Number]%** shall not constitute a breach.

## 4. Scope of Restriction

### 4.1 Activity Scope

The restriction applies only to activities substantially similar to those performed by the Restricted Party during the last **[Number] months** of engagement.

### 4.2 Geographical Scope

The restriction applies within **[Territory]**.

### 4.3 Restricted Period

The non-compete obligation applies for **[Number] months** following termination.

## 5. Compensation (If Required by Law)

### 5.1 Statutory Compensation

Where mandatory law requires compensation for post-termination non-compete obligations, the Company shall pay compensation in accordance with applicable legislation.

### 5.2 Mitigation

Compensation shall be reduced or discontinued where legally permissible if the Restricted Party obtains other suitable employment.

## 6. Garden Leave (Optional)

The Company may place the Restricted Party on garden leave during the notice period, during which salary shall continue and competitive activities remain prohibited.

## 7. Confidential Information

Nothing in this Agreement limits the Restricted Party’s ongoing obligation to maintain confidentiality of the Company’s proprietary information.

## 8. Non-Solicitation (Optional Clause)

For a period of **[Number] months** following termination, the Restricted Party shall not:
- solicit or attempt to solicit the Company’s customers with whom the Restricted Party had material contact
- solicit or hire the Company’s employees

## 9. Reasonableness and Severability

### 9.1 Reasonableness

The Parties agree that the restrictions are reasonable and necessary to protect legitimate business interests.

### 9.2 Severability

If any provision is deemed invalid or unenforceable, it shall be modified to the minimum extent necessary to make it enforceable.

## 10. Remedies

### 10.1 Injunctive Relief

The Company shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in case of breach.

### 10.2 Damages

The Company may claim damages in accordance with applicable law.

## 11. Termination of Restriction

The Company may waive the non-compete obligation in writing, in which case compensation obligations shall cease in accordance with applicable law.

## 12. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[Location]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 13. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding post-engagement competition restrictions. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 14. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
