**Employee**
- Full legal name: **[COMPANY NAME]**
- Address: **[ADDRESS]**

The Employer and the Employee are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Position and Duties

### 2.1 Position

The Employee is employed as **[JOB TITLE]**.

### 2.2 Responsibilities

The Employee shall perform duties associated with the position and any other reasonable tasks assigned by the Employer consistent with the Employee’s qualifications.

### 2.3 Loyalty and Conduct

The Employee shall act loyally and in the best interests of the Employer and comply with internal policies and applicable laws.

## 3. Term of Employment

### 3.1 Fixed Term

Employment commences on **[DATE]** and automatically terminates on **[DATE]**, without further notice, unless extended in writing.

### 3.2 Reason for Fixed Term (If Required by Law)

The fixed term is justified by **[OBJECTIVE REASON]**.

### 3.3 No Expectation of Renewal

Expiration of the fixed term shall not be considered termination unless required by mandatory law.

### 3.4 Conversion to Permanent Employment

If the Employee continues working beyond the end date without a new written agreement, the employment may be deemed indefinite in accordance with applicable law.

## 4. Working Hours

### 4.1 Normal Hours

The Employee’s normal working hours are **[NUMBER]** hours per week.

### 4.2 Additional Work

The Employee may be required to perform reasonable additional work in line with operational needs. Overtime compensation applies only if required by law or expressly agreed.

## 5. Remuneration

### 5.1 Salary

The Employee shall receive a fixed gross salary of **[NUMBER] [CURRENCY]** per month.

### 5.2 Payment

Salary shall be paid monthly in arrears to the Employee’s designated bank account.

### 5.3 Variable Compensation (If Applicable)

Any bonus or incentive arrangement shall be governed by separate written terms and shall not be guaranteed unless expressly stated.

### 5.4 Benefits

The Employee shall be entitled to benefits equivalent to comparable permanent employees unless otherwise permitted by law. Details are set out in Schedule 1.

## 6. Holidays and Leave

### 6.1 Holiday Entitlement

The Employee is entitled to holiday and holiday pay in accordance with applicable law.

### 6.2 Statutory Leave

The Employee is entitled to statutory leave, including sickness and parental leave, subject to applicable legislation.

### 6.3 Illness Reporting

Illness must be reported without undue delay in accordance with company procedures.

## 7. Confidentiality

### 7.1 Confidential Information

The Employee shall not disclose confidential information relating to the Employer’s business, clients, suppliers, or employees.

### 7.2 Survival

This obligation continues after termination of employment without limitation in time.

## 8. Intellectual Property

### 8.1 Ownership

All work results, inventions, materials, and intellectual property created by the Employee in the course of employment shall belong exclusively to the Employer to the extent permitted by law.

### 8.2 Assignment

The Employee assigns all transferable rights to such intellectual property to the Employer without additional compensation unless otherwise required by law.

## 9. Non-Competition and Non-Solicitation (If Applicable)

### 9.1 Separate Terms

Any post-employment restrictions must comply with mandatory employment legislation and shall be separately specified in Schedule 2, if applicable.

### 9.2 Statutory Compensation

Where statutory compensation is required for such restrictions, it shall be paid in accordance with applicable law.

## 10. Secondary Employment

The Employee may not undertake secondary employment or business activities that conflict with the Employer’s legitimate interests without prior written consent.

## 11. Company Property

All equipment, documents, and materials provided by the Employer remain the Employer’s property and shall be returned upon expiration or termination of employment.

## 12. Data Protection

The Employer processes personal data relating to the Employee in accordance with applicable data protection legislation and internal policies.

## 13. Early Termination

### 13.1 Notice

Unless otherwise required by law, either Party may terminate the Agreement before the expiry date with written notice of **[NUMBER] [TIME UNIT]**.

### 13.2 Immediate Termination

Immediate termination may occur in case of material breach or gross misconduct, subject to applicable law.

### 13.3 Accrued Rights

Any termination before expiry shall not affect accrued rights or obligations.

## 14. Liability

The Employee is liable for losses caused by intentional misconduct or gross negligence, subject to applicable law.

## 15. Assignment

The Employee may not assign rights or obligations under this Agreement. The Employer may transfer this Agreement as part of a business transfer, merger, or restructuring.

## 16. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[LOCATION]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 17. Language

This Agreement is executed in English, which shall prevail in case of inconsistency.

## 18. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes prior agreements relating to the employment. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 19. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
