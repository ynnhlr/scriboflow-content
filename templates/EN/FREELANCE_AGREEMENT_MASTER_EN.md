# Freelance Agreement

This Freelance Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "Effective Date").

## 1. Parties

**Client**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF CLIENT]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY/INDIVIDUAL]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**

**Freelancer**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF FREELANCER]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY/INDIVIDUAL]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**

The Client and the Freelancer are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Scope of Services

### 2.1 Services

The Freelancer shall provide the services described in Schedule 1 (the "Services").

### 2.2 Performance

The Services shall be performed independently and in accordance with the agreed specifications and deadlines.

### 2.3 Scope Changes

Any changes to scope must be agreed in writing. Work outside the agreed scope may be subject to additional fees.

## 3. Independent Contractor Status

### 3.1 Independent Contractor

The Freelancer acts as an independent contractor and not as an employee, partner, or agent of the Client.

### 3.2 Freelancer Responsibilities

The Freelancer is responsible for:
- taxes and social contributions
- insurance coverage
- statutory obligations applicable to self-employed persons

### 3.3 No Employment Relationship

Nothing in this Agreement shall create an employment relationship.

## 4. Fees and Payment

### 4.1 Compensation

The Client shall pay the Freelancer **[HOURLY RATE]** or **[FIXED PROJECT FEE]**, as specified in Schedule 1.

### 4.2 Invoicing

The Freelancer shall invoice the Client in accordance with agreed milestones or monthly in arrears.

### 4.3 Payment Terms

Invoices are payable within **[X]** days from receipt unless otherwise agreed.

### 4.4 Late Payment

Late payments may accrue statutory interest.

## 5. Expenses

The Freelancer shall bear all costs related to performing the Services unless expressly agreed otherwise in writing.

## 6. Intellectual Property

### 6.1 Ownership

All deliverables and work products created specifically for the Client under this Agreement (the "Work Product") shall become the Client’s property upon full payment.

### 6.2 Assignment

To the extent permitted by law, the Freelancer assigns all transferable intellectual property rights in the Work Product to the Client.

### 6.3 Pre-Existing Materials

Any pre-existing materials remain the Freelancer’s property unless expressly transferred in writing.

### 6.4 Portfolio Use

Unless otherwise agreed, the Freelancer may reference the project for portfolio purposes, provided no confidential information is disclosed.

## 7. Confidentiality

### 7.1 Confidential Information

The Freelancer shall not disclose confidential information obtained in connection with this Agreement.

### 7.2 Survival

This obligation continues after termination without limitation in time.

## 8. Non-Solicitation (Optional)

During the term of this Agreement and for **[X months]** thereafter, the Freelancer shall not solicit the Client’s employees or customers for competing services without prior written consent.

## 9. Warranties

### 9.1 Freelancer Warranties

The Freelancer warrants that:
- the Services shall be performed with professional skill and care
- the Work Product does not infringe third-party rights

### 9.2 Remedy

The Freelancer shall remedy defects notified within a reasonable time.

## 10. Liability

### 10.1 Liability for Breach

Each Party shall be liable for damages caused by breach of this Agreement.

### 10.2 Liability Cap

The Freelancer’s total liability shall not exceed **[X amount or fee paid]**, except in cases of gross negligence, fraud, or wilful misconduct.

### 10.3 Indirect Damages

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages unless required by law.

## 11. Term and Termination

### 11.1 Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until completion of the Services unless terminated earlier.

### 11.2 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate with **[X days’]** written notice.

### 11.3 Termination for Cause

The Client may terminate immediately in case of material breach.

### 11.4 Final Deliverables

Upon termination, the Freelancer shall deliver completed work and invoice for work performed up to the termination date.

## 12. Data Protection

If the Freelancer processes personal data on behalf of the Client, the Parties shall enter into a separate Data Processing Agreement where required.

## 13. Assignment

The Freelancer may not assign rights or obligations under this Agreement without prior written consent. The Client may assign this Agreement in connection with a business transfer or restructuring.

## 14. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[CITY, DENMARK]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 15. Language

This Agreement is executed in English, which shall prevail in case of inconsistency.

## 16. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings relating to the Services. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 17. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
