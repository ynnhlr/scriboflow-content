# Consulting Agreement

This Consulting Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "Effective Date").

## 1. Parties

**Client**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF CLIENT]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY/INDIVIDUAL]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**

**Consultant**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF CONSULTANT]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY/INDIVIDUAL]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**

The Client and the Consultant are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Services

### 2.1 Services

The Consultant shall provide the services described in Schedule 1 (the "Services").

### 2.2 Standard of Performance

The Services shall be performed with reasonable skill, care, and diligence consistent with industry standards.

### 2.3 Scope Changes

Any changes to the scope must be agreed in writing. Work performed outside the agreed scope may result in additional fees.

## 3. Term

### 3.1 Commencement

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date.

### 3.2 Duration

The Agreement shall continue until completion of the Services or termination in accordance with Section 12.

## 4. Fees and Payment

### 4.1 Compensation Structure

The Client shall pay the Consultant **[HOURLY RATE]** or **[FIXED PROJECT FEE]**, as specified in Schedule 1.

### 4.2 Invoicing

Invoices shall be issued **[MONTHLY / PER MILESTONE / UPON COMPLETION]**.

### 4.3 Payment Terms

Invoices are payable within **[X]** days from receipt.

### 4.4 Late Payment

Late payments may accrue statutory interest and reasonable collection costs.

### 4.5 Disputed Amounts

Undisputed portions of invoices must be paid on time even if part of the invoice is disputed.

## 5. Expenses

The Consultant shall bear all expenses unless explicitly approved in writing by the Client.

## 6. Independent Contractor Status

### 6.1 Independent Contractor

The Consultant acts as an independent contractor.

### 6.2 No Employment Relationship

Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, or agency relationship.

### 6.3 Taxes

The Consultant is responsible for all taxes, social contributions, and statutory obligations.

## 7. Intellectual Property

### 7.1 Work Product Ownership

All deliverables specifically created for the Client (the "Work Product") shall become the Client’s property upon full payment.

### 7.2 Assignment

The Consultant assigns all transferable intellectual property rights in the Work Product to the Client upon payment.

### 7.3 Pre-Existing Materials

The Consultant retains ownership of pre-existing materials but grants the Client a non-exclusive, perpetual license to use such materials as integrated into the Work Product.

## 8. Confidentiality

### 8.1 Confidential Information

Each Party shall treat confidential information received from the other Party as strictly confidential.

### 8.2 Survival

This obligation survives termination of the Agreement.

## 9. Warranties

### 9.1 Consultant Warranties

The Consultant warrants that:
- the Services shall be performed professionally
- the Work Product shall not knowingly infringe third-party rights

### 9.2 Disclaimer

Except as expressly stated, no additional warranties are given.

## 10. Limitation of Liability

### 10.1 Indirect Damages

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages unless required by law.

### 10.2 Liability Cap

The Consultant’s total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement, except in cases of fraud, gross negligence, or wilful misconduct.

## 11. Indemnification

The Consultant shall indemnify the Client against third-party claims arising from:
- intellectual property infringement caused by the Work Product
- breach of this Agreement

## 12. Termination

### 12.1 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **[X days’]** written notice.

### 12.2 Termination for Cause

The Client may terminate immediately in case of material breach.

### 12.3 Effects of Termination

Upon termination:
- the Consultant shall deliver completed work
- the Client shall pay for Services performed up to the termination date

## 13. Data Protection

If the Consultant processes personal data on behalf of the Client, the Parties shall enter into a separate Data Processing Agreement.

## 14. Assignment

The Consultant may not assign rights or obligations without prior written consent. The Client may assign this Agreement in connection with a merger, acquisition, or business transfer.

## 15. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[CITY, DENMARK]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 16. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes prior agreements relating to the Services. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 17. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
