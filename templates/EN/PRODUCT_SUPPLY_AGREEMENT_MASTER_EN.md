### 2.3 Changes

## 3. Term

### 3.1 Commencement

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date.

### 3.2 Initial Term

The Agreement shall continue for an initial term of **[Number] years**, unless terminated in accordance with Section 13.

### 3.3 Renewal

Thereafter, it shall automatically renew unless terminated with **[Number] months** written notice.

## 4. Orders

### 4.1 Purchase Orders

The Buyer shall submit purchase orders specifying quantities and delivery dates.

### 4.2 Acceptance

Orders become binding upon written confirmation by the Supplier.

### 4.3 Rejection

The Supplier may reject orders if production capacity or commercial conditions do not allow acceptance.

## 5. Pricing

### 5.1 Prices

Prices are set out in Schedule 2.

### 5.2 Adjustments

Prices may be adjusted upon **[Number] days** written notice due to:
- changes in raw material costs
- exchange rate fluctuations
- regulatory changes

### 5.3 Taxes

All prices are exclusive of VAT unless otherwise stated.

## 6. Payment Terms

### 6.1 Payment

Invoices are payable within **[Number] days** from invoice date.

### 6.2 Late Payment

Late payments may accrue statutory interest.

### 6.3 Suspension

The Supplier may suspend deliveries in case of overdue payments.

## 7. Delivery

### 7.1 Delivery Terms

Delivery shall take place in accordance with **[INCOTERM] [Location]**.

### 7.2 Delivery Times

Delivery times are estimates unless expressly agreed as fixed.

### 7.3 Risk

Risk passes in accordance with the agreed Incoterm.

### 7.4 Partial Deliveries

Partial deliveries are permitted unless otherwise agreed.

## 8. Quality and Inspection

### 8.1 Inspection

The Buyer shall inspect Products promptly upon delivery.

### 8.2 Complaint Period

Complaints must be submitted in writing within **[Number] days** of delivery.

### 8.3 Remedies

In case of defects, the Supplier may, at its discretion:
- repair
- replace
- refund defective Products

## 9. Compliance

### 9.1 Product Compliance

The Supplier warrants that Products comply with applicable safety and regulatory standards at the time of delivery.

### 9.2 Resale Compliance

The Buyer is responsible for compliance related to resale, labeling, and marketing within its territory.

## 10. Intellectual Property

### 10.1 Supplier IP

All intellectual property related to the Products remains the property of the Supplier unless otherwise agreed.

### 10.2 Buyer Specifications

If Products are manufactured according to Buyer’s specifications, the Buyer warrants that such specifications do not infringe third-party rights.

## 11. Indemnification

### 11.1 Supplier Indemnity

The Supplier shall indemnify the Buyer against third-party claims arising from product defects caused by the Supplier.

### 11.2 Buyer Indemnity

The Buyer shall indemnify the Supplier for claims arising from misuse, improper storage, or unauthorized modification of the Products.

## 12. Limitation of Liability

### 12.1 Indirect Damages

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages, including loss of profit.

### 12.2 Liability Cap

The Supplier’s total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total value of Products delivered during the preceding **[Number] months**, except in cases of gross negligence or wilful misconduct.

## 13. Termination

### 13.1 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **[Number] months** written notice.

### 13.2 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate immediately in case of material breach not remedied within **[Number] days**.

### 13.3 Effects

Upon termination, all outstanding payments become due.

## 14. Force Majeure

Neither Party shall be liable for failure to perform obligations due to events beyond reasonable control, including supply chain disruptions or governmental actions.

## 15. Confidentiality

Each Party shall keep confidential all commercial and technical information received from the other Party.

## 16. Assignment

Neither Party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent, except in connection with a merger or business transfer.

## 17. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[Location]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 18. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the supply of Products. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 19. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
