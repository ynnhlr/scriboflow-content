# Articles of Association

**Company name:** **[COMPANY NAME]**  
**CVR No.:** **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**  
**Adopted on:** **[DATE]**

## 1. Name

The name of the Company is **[COMPANY NAME]**.

## 2. Registered Office

The registered office of the Company is located in **[MUNICIPALITY]**, Denmark.

## 3. Purpose

The purpose of the Company is:
- **[Describe business activities]**
- any activities related thereto

## 4. Share Capital

### 4.1 Amount

The share capital of the Company amounts to **[AMOUNT]** **[CURRENCY]**.

### 4.2 Nominal Value

The share capital is divided into shares of **[NOMINAL VALUE]** each.

### 4.3 Paid-In

All shares are fully paid unless otherwise stated.

## 5. Share Classes (If Applicable)

### 5.1 Classes

The Company may issue different share classes as defined in Schedule 1.

### 5.2 Rights

Rights attached to share classes may include:
- voting rights
- dividend rights
- liquidation preferences
- redemption rights

### 5.3 Variations

Any variation of rights requires approval in accordance with applicable law.

## 6. Transfer of Shares

### 6.1 Approval

Shares may not be transferred without prior approval of the general meeting unless otherwise provided by law.

### 6.2 Right of First Refusal

Existing shareholders shall have a right of first refusal on any proposed transfer.

### 6.3 Register

The board of directors shall ensure that the shareholder register is updated upon valid transfer.

## 7. General Meeting

### 7.1 Authority

The general meeting is the supreme authority of the Company.

### 7.2 Annual General Meeting

The annual general meeting shall be held within **[X months]** after the end of each financial year.

### 7.3 Notice

Notice of general meetings shall be given in accordance with applicable law.

### 7.4 Voting

Each share of **[NOMINAL VALUE]** carries one vote unless otherwise provided in Section 5.

## 8. Management

### 8.1 Structure

The Company shall be managed by:
- a board of directors and executive management, or
- executive management only (if permitted by law)

### 8.2 Appointment

Members of management are appointed by the general meeting unless otherwise required by law.

### 8.3 Rules of Procedure

The board of directors shall adopt rules of procedure if applicable.

## 9. Signing Authority

The Company shall be bound by:
- **[Two directors jointly]**, or
- **[One director together with the CEO]**, or
- **[Specify signing structure]**

## 10. Financial Year

The financial year of the Company runs from **[START DATE]** to **[END DATE]**.

## 11. Dividends

### 11.1 Distribution

Dividends shall be distributed in accordance with applicable company law.

### 11.2 Decision

The general meeting decides on distribution based on recommendation from management.

## 12. Electronic Communication

The Company may use electronic communication in accordance with applicable law.

## 13. Amendments

Amendments to these Articles of Association require approval by the general meeting in accordance with applicable law.

## 14. Dissolution

The Company may be dissolved by resolution of the general meeting in accordance with applicable law.
