**Consignor**
- Full legal name: **[Company name]**
- Registered address: **[Address]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[CVR]**

**Consignee**
- Full legal name: **[Company name]**
- Registered address: **[Address]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[CVR]**

The Consignor and the Consignee are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Purpose

The Consignor appoints the Consignee to hold, market, and sell certain goods owned by the Consignor on a consignment basis under this Agreement. No transfer of ownership is intended except as expressly provided herein.

## 3. Consigned Goods

### 3.1 Definition

"Consigned Goods" means the goods described in Schedule 1, including quantities, specifications, and recommended resale prices.

### 3.2 Ownership

Title to the Consigned Goods remains with the Consignor until the goods are sold to an end customer and full payment has been received.

### 3.3 Identification and Segregation

The Consignee shall clearly identify the Consigned Goods as belonging to the Consignor and, unless otherwise agreed, keep them separate from other inventory.

## 4. Delivery and Risk

### 4.1 Delivery

Delivery terms, including location and logistics responsibility, are set out in Schedule 2.

### 4.2 Risk of Loss

Risk of loss or damage passes to the Consignee upon receipt and remains with the Consignee until the goods are sold or returned to the Consignor, except where loss is caused by force majeure.

## 5. Sale of Consigned Goods

### 5.1 Authority to Sell

The Consignee is authorised to sell the Consigned Goods in its own name, but on behalf of the Consignor.

### 5.2 Pricing and Commercial Terms

The Consignee shall comply with the pricing and commercial conditions set out in Schedule 1 and shall not discount, bundle, or otherwise alter the sale terms without prior written consent, unless expressly permitted.

### 5.3 Restrictions

The Consignee shall not pledge, assign, lease, or otherwise encumber the Consigned Goods.

## 6. Reporting and Records

### 6.1 Sales Reports

The Consignee shall provide written sales reports at **[Number]** intervals, detailing:
- units sold
- sale prices
- returns
- remaining inventory

### 6.2 Inspection and Audit

Upon reasonable notice, the Consignor may inspect records relating to the Consigned Goods to verify compliance with this Agreement.

## 7. Commission and Payment

### 7.1 Commission

The Consignee shall be entitled to a commission of **[Number]% / [Number] [Currency]** per unit sold, as specified in Schedule 1.

### 7.2 Settlement

Payment of amounts due to the Consignor shall be made no later than **[Number]** days after the end of each reporting period.

### 7.3 Condition for Commission

Commission is earned only upon completed sale and receipt of payment from the end customer.

### 7.4 No Set-Off

The Consignee may not offset payments unless expressly agreed in writing.

## 8. Returns and Unsold Goods

### 8.1 Customer Returns

Customer returns shall be handled in accordance with Schedule 3. Returned goods remain the property of the Consignor.

### 8.2 Unsold Goods

Upon termination or written request, unsold Consigned Goods shall be returned to the Consignor within **[Number]** days, at the Consignee’s expense, in reasonable saleable condition.

## 9. Care, Insurance, and Compliance

### 9.1 Standard of Care

The Consignee shall exercise commercially reasonable care in storing, handling, and displaying the Consigned Goods.

### 9.2 Insurance

Unless otherwise agreed, the Consignee shall maintain adequate insurance covering loss, theft, and damage of the Consigned Goods.

### 9.3 Legal Compliance

The Consignee shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations relating to the marketing and sale of the Consigned Goods.

## 10. Intellectual Property

All trademarks, product names, designs, and marketing materials related to the Consigned Goods remain the exclusive property of the Consignor. No rights are granted except the limited right to market and sell the Consigned Goods during the term of this Agreement.

## 11. Term and Termination

### 11.1 Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until terminated.

### 11.2 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **[Number]** days’ written notice.

### 11.3 Immediate Termination

Either Party may terminate immediately in case of material breach, insolvency, or misuse of the Consigned Goods. Termination shall not affect accrued payment obligations.

## 12. Liability and Indemnification

### 12.1 Indemnification

The Consignee shall indemnify the Consignor against losses arising from negligence, unauthorised sales practices, or breach of this Agreement.

### 12.2 Limitation of Liability

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages, except in cases of fraud or wilful misconduct.

## 13. Confidentiality

All non-public information exchanged under this Agreement shall be treated as confidential and shall not be disclosed without prior written consent, unless required by law.

## 14. Assignment

Neither Party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent, except in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets.

## 15. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[Location]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 16. Language

This Agreement is executed in English, which shall prevail in case of inconsistency.

## 17. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding the subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions or agreements. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 18. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
