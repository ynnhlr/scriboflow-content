This Loan Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[DATE]** (the "Effective Date").

## 1. Parties

**Lender**
- Full legal name: **[COMPANY NAME]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No. / CPR No.: **[CVR]**

**Borrower**
- Full legal name: **[COMPANY NAME]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No. / CPR No.: **[CVR]**

The Lender and the Borrower are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Loan Amount

### 2.1 Principal

The Lender agrees to lend the Borrower the principal amount of **[NUMBER] [CURRENCY]** (the "Loan Amount").

### 2.2 Disbursement

The Loan Amount shall be transferred to the Borrower on or before **[DATE]** to the account designated by the Borrower.

## 3. Purpose of the Loan (Optional)

The Loan shall be used for **[PURPOSE]**. The Borrower shall not use the Loan for unlawful purposes.

## 4. Interest

### 4.1 Interest Rate

The Loan shall bear interest at:
- **[NUMBER]% per annum**, or
- **[NUMBER]%**, or
- **Interest-free (if applicable)**

### 4.2 Accrual

Interest shall accrue from the date of disbursement until full repayment.

### 4.3 Payment of Interest

Interest shall be payable **[PAYMENT FREQUENCY]**.

## 5. Repayment

### 5.1 Repayment Schedule

The Borrower shall repay the Loan Amount as follows:
- **[PAYMENT TERMS]**, or
- full repayment on **[DATE]**

### 5.2 Payment Account

Payments shall be made to the following account:
- Bank: **[BANK NAME]**
- IBAN: **[IBAN]**
- Reference: **[REFERENCE]**

## 6. Early Repayment

The Borrower may prepay the Loan in full or in part without penalty unless otherwise agreed.

## 7. Default

### 7.1 Events of Default

The Borrower shall be in default if:
- any payment is overdue by more than **[NUMBER] days**
- the Borrower becomes insolvent
- the Borrower breaches material obligations under this Agreement

### 7.2 Lender Remedies

Upon default, the Lender may:
- declare the entire outstanding principal and accrued interest immediately due and payable
- claim statutory default interest
- initiate legal recovery proceedings

## 8. Security (Optional)

### 8.1 Security Provided

As security for repayment, the Borrower provides:
- **[PERSONAL GUARANTEE]**
- **[PLEDGE OVER ASSETS]**
- **[SECURITY TYPE]**

Details are set out in Schedule 1.

### 8.2 Enforcement

The Lender may enforce security in accordance with applicable law upon default.

## 9. Representations and Warranties

### 9.1 Borrower Representations

The Borrower represents that:
- it has authority to enter into this Agreement
- the Agreement constitutes a legally binding obligation
- it is not insolvent at the time of signing

## 10. Costs

The Borrower shall bear reasonable costs related to enforcement of this Agreement in case of default.

## 11. Assignment

The Borrower may not assign rights or obligations under this Agreement without written consent. The Lender may assign its rights in connection with refinancing or business transfer.

## 12. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[LOCATION]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 13. Severability

If any provision is found invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.

## 14. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the Loan. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 15. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
