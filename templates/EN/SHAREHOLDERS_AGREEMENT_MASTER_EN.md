## 2. Purpose

### 2.1 Relationship

This Agreement governs the relationship between the Shareholders and the Company.

### 2.2 Articles Alignment

In case of conflict between this Agreement and the Company’s Articles of Association, the Parties shall take necessary steps to align the Articles with this Agreement.

## 3. Share Capital and Ownership

### 3.1 Ownership

The share capital of the Company is divided as set out in Schedule 1.

### 3.2 New Shares

No new shares may be issued without approval in accordance with Section 6.

## 4. Governance

### 4.1 Management

The Company shall be managed by a board of directors and/or executive management in accordance with applicable law.

### 4.2 Reserved Matters

The following matters require approval of **[Approval threshold]**:
- issuance of new shares
- amendments to Articles of Association
- sale of material assets
- mergers or liquidation
- incurrence of significant debt
- entry into transactions outside ordinary course of business

## 5. Dividend Policy

Dividends shall be declared in accordance with applicable law and based on the Company’s financial position and strategic needs.

## 6. Transfer of Shares

### 6.1 Approval

Shares may not be transferred without prior written approval of **[Approval threshold]** Shareholders.

### 6.2 Right of First Refusal

Any proposed transfer shall first be offered to existing Shareholders on a pro rata basis (the "Right of First Refusal").

### 6.3 Invalid Transfers

Transfers in violation of this Agreement shall be null and void.

## 7. Drag-Along Rights

If Shareholders holding at least **[Number]%** of the shares agree to sell the Company to a third party, they may require remaining Shareholders to sell their shares on the same terms ("Drag-Along").

## 8. Tag-Along Rights

If one or more Shareholders propose to sell shares representing more than **[Number]%** of the share capital, the remaining Shareholders shall have the right to participate in the sale on the same terms ("Tag-Along").

## 9. Deadlock

### 9.1 Negotiation

In the event of a deadlock on reserved matters, the Parties shall attempt resolution through negotiation.

### 9.2 Mediation

If unresolved within **[Number] days**, the matter shall be escalated to mediation.

### 9.3 Buy-Sell or Dissolution

If still unresolved, the Parties may:
- trigger a buy-sell mechanism, or
- dissolve the Company, subject to applicable law

## 10. Confidentiality

The Shareholders shall keep confidential all non-public information relating to the Company.

## 11. Non-Competition and Non-Solicitation (Optional)

During their shareholding and for **[Number] months** thereafter, Shareholders actively involved in the business shall not compete with the Company. Shareholders shall not solicit employees or customers. Such restrictions shall comply with applicable law.

## 12. Exit and Buyout Events

### 12.1 Trigger Events

In case of death, incapacity, or material breach by a Shareholder, the remaining Shareholders may purchase the affected Shareholder’s shares.

### 12.2 Valuation

Share price shall be determined by:
- **[Valuation method]**, or
- **[Valuation method]**

Details are set out in Schedule 2.

## 13. Funding Obligations

### 13.1 Pro Rata Participation

If additional funding is required, Shareholders shall have the right, but not the obligation, to participate pro rata.

### 13.2 Dilution

Failure to participate may result in dilution.

## 14. Liability

No Shareholder shall be liable beyond their capital contribution unless otherwise agreed.

## 15. Term

This Agreement remains in force as long as two or more Shareholders hold shares in the Company.

## 16. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[Location]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 17. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning shareholder relations. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by all Shareholders.

## 18. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
