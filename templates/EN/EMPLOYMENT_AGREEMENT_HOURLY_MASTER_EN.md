This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[Date]** (the "Effective Date").

## 1. Parties

**Employer**
- Full legal name: **[Company name]**
- Registered address: **[Address]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[CVR]**

**Employee**
- Full legal name: **[Company name]**
- Address: **[Address]**
- CPR/ID No.: **[CVR]**

The Employer and the Employee are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Position and Duties

### 2.1 Position

The Employee is employed in the position of **[Job title]**.

### 2.2 Duties

The Employee shall perform the duties reasonably associated with the position, as well as other tasks assigned by the Employer within the Employee’s qualifications.

### 2.3 Best Efforts

The Employee shall perform duties loyally, diligently, and in the best interests of the Employer.

## 3. Commencement and Duration

### 3.1 Start Date

Employment commences on **[Date]**.

### 3.2 Duration

The employment is **[Term type] until [Date]**. No minimum number of working hours is guaranteed unless expressly stated in this Agreement.

## 4. Working Hours

### 4.1 Hourly Employment

The Employee is engaged on an hourly basis.

### 4.2 Working Time

Working hours shall be scheduled by the Employer based on operational needs and may vary from week to week.

### 4.3 No Exclusivity of Hours

Unless otherwise agreed, the Employer is not obliged to offer a minimum number of hours.

## 5. Remuneration

### 5.1 Hourly Wage

The Employee shall receive an hourly wage of **[Number] [Currency]** before tax.

### 5.2 Payment

Wages shall be paid **[Number]** in arrears to the Employee’s designated bank account.

### 5.3 Overtime

Overtime compensation applies only if expressly agreed in writing or required by mandatory law.

## 6. Registration and Time Tracking

### 6.1 Time Recording

The Employee shall accurately record all working hours using the Employer’s designated system.

### 6.2 Approval

Payment is conditional upon correct and timely registration and approval of hours worked.

## 7. Holidays and Absence

### 7.1 Holiday Entitlement

The Employee is entitled to holiday in accordance with applicable law.

### 7.2 Sickness

In case of illness, the Employee shall notify the Employer as soon as reasonably possible and comply with applicable reporting requirements.

### 7.3 Unauthorised Absence

Unauthorised absence may result in wage deductions and disciplinary action.

## 8. Confidentiality

### 8.1 Confidential Information

During and after employment, the Employee shall not disclose any confidential or proprietary information relating to the Employer, its clients, or business operations.

### 8.2 Survival

This obligation survives termination of employment without time limitation.

## 9. Intellectual Property

### 9.1 Work Product

All work results, materials, inventions, and intellectual property created by the Employee in the course of employment shall be the exclusive property of the Employer.

### 9.2 Assignment

To the extent permitted by law, the Employee assigns all such rights to the Employer without additional compensation.

## 10. Secondary Employment

The Employee shall not engage in secondary employment that conflicts with the Employer’s business or interests without prior written consent.

## 11. Equipment and Property

Any equipment, documents, or materials provided by the Employer remain the Employer’s property and shall be returned upon request or termination.

## 12. Termination

### 12.1 Notice

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **[Number] [Time unit]** written notice, unless otherwise required by mandatory law.

### 12.2 Immediate Termination

The Employer may terminate employment with immediate effect in case of material breach, gross misconduct, or serious violation of obligations.

### 12.3 Final Settlement

Upon termination, all outstanding wages shall be settled in accordance with applicable law.

## 13. Liability

The Employee is liable for losses caused by intentional misconduct or gross negligence, subject to applicable law.

## 14. Data Protection

Personal data relating to the Employee shall be processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws and the Employer’s internal policies.

## 15. Assignment

The Employee may not assign rights or obligations under this Agreement. The Employer may assign this Agreement as part of a business transfer or restructuring.

## 16. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[Location]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 17. Language

This Agreement is executed in English, which shall prevail in case of inconsistency.

## 18. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 19. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
