# Distribution Agreement

This Distribution Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "Effective Date").

## 1. Parties

**Supplier**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF SUPPLIER]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY TYPE]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR No.: **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**

**Distributor**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF DISTRIBUTOR]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY TYPE]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR No.: **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**

The Supplier and the Distributor are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Appointment

### 2.1 Appointment

The Supplier appoints the Distributor to market and resell the products described in Schedule 1 (the "Products") within the Territory defined below.

### 2.2 Acceptance

The Distributor accepts the appointment and agrees to actively promote and sell the Products.

### 2.3 Exclusivity

This Agreement is **[NON-EXCLUSIVE / EXCLUSIVE]** within the Territory.

## 3. Territory

### 3.1 Territory

The Distributor is authorised to distribute the Products within **[DEFINE GEOGRAPHICAL AREA]** (the "Territory").

### 3.2 Outside Territory

The Distributor shall not actively sell outside the Territory without prior written consent.

### 3.3 Passive Sales

Passive sales into other territories shall be handled in accordance with applicable competition law.

## 4. Orders and Acceptance

### 4.1 Orders

All orders submitted by the Distributor are subject to written acceptance by the Supplier.

### 4.2 Rejection

The Supplier may reject orders at its discretion.

### 4.3 Governing Terms

Each accepted order constitutes a binding sales contract governed by this Agreement and the Supplier’s General Terms and Conditions of Sale.

## 5. Prices and Payment

### 5.1 Prices

The Distributor shall purchase Products at the wholesale prices listed in Schedule 2.

### 5.2 Price Changes

The Supplier may adjust prices upon **[X days’]** written notice.

### 5.3 Payment Terms

Payment terms are **[X days]** from invoice date unless otherwise agreed.

### 5.4 Late Payment

Late payments may result in suspension of deliveries.

## 6. Marketing and Promotion

### 6.1 Distributor Obligations

The Distributor shall:
- actively market the Products in the Territory
- maintain adequate sales resources
- comply with brand guidelines provided by the Supplier

### 6.2 Representations

The Distributor shall not make representations about the Products beyond those approved by the Supplier.

## 7. Minimum Performance (Optional)

### 7.1 Targets

The Distributor shall achieve minimum annual purchase volumes as defined in Schedule 3.

### 7.2 Consequences

Failure to meet minimum targets may result in:
- termination of exclusivity, or
- termination of this Agreement

## 8. Intellectual Property

### 8.1 Ownership

All trademarks, trade names, and branding remain the property of the Supplier.

### 8.2 License

The Distributor is granted a limited, non-transferable right to use such intellectual property solely for marketing the Products during the term of this Agreement.

### 8.3 Post-Termination

Upon termination, all use of intellectual property must cease immediately.

## 9. Warranties and Liability

### 9.1 Supplier Warranty

The Supplier warrants that the Products comply with applicable laws at the time of delivery.

### 9.2 Limitation

The Supplier’s liability shall be limited in accordance with its General Terms and Conditions of Sale.

### 9.3 Local Compliance

The Distributor is responsible for compliance with local marketing and resale regulations within the Territory.

## 10. Product Liability

### 10.1 Fault

Each Party shall be liable for damages caused by its own fault.

### 10.2 Notice

The Distributor shall promptly notify the Supplier of any product claims.

### 10.3 Admissions

The Distributor shall not admit liability on behalf of the Supplier without prior written consent.

## 11. Term and Termination

### 11.1 Initial Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of **[X years]**.

### 11.2 Renewal

Thereafter, it shall automatically renew unless terminated with **[X months’]** written notice.

### 11.3 Immediate Termination

Either Party may terminate immediately in case of:
- material breach not remedied within **[X days]**
- insolvency
- serious violation of brand or competition obligations

## 12. Consequences of Termination

### 12.1 Effects

Upon termination:
- all outstanding payments become due
- the Distributor shall cease representing itself as authorised distributor
- remaining stock handling shall be agreed in writing

### 12.2 Compensation

No goodwill or compensation shall be payable unless required by mandatory law.

## 13. Confidentiality

Each Party shall treat commercial and technical information received from the other Party as confidential.

## 14. Competition Law Compliance

### 14.1 Compliance

The Parties shall comply with applicable competition and antitrust laws.

### 14.2 Interpretation

Nothing in this Agreement shall be interpreted as restricting lawful competition beyond what is permitted under applicable law.

## 15. Force Majeure

Neither Party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable control.

## 16. Assignment

Neither Party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent, except in connection with a merger or business transfer.

## 17. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[CITY, DENMARK]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 18. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning distribution of the Products. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 19. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
