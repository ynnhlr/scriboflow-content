**Company**
- Full legal name: **[Company name]**
- Entity type: **[Company type]**
- Registered address: **[Address]**
- CVR No.: **[CVR]**

**Assignor**
- Full legal name: **[Company name]**
- Address: **[Address]**
- CPR/CVR No.: **[CVR]**

The Company and the Assignor are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Background

### 2.1 Creation

The Assignor has created or contributed to certain intellectual property.

### 2.2 Transfer Intent

The Parties wish to ensure that ownership of such intellectual property is transferred to the Company.

## 3. Definitions

"Intellectual Property" means all rights in and to:
- inventions
- patents and patent applications
- trademarks and trade names
- copyrights and related rights
- software and source code
- databases
- designs
- know-how and trade secrets
- any other proprietary rights

whether registered or unregistered.

## 4. Assignment of Rights

### 4.1 Assignment

The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns and transfers to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to:
- all Intellectual Property created in connection with **[Employment / engagement / project name]**
- all derivative works and improvements thereof

### 4.2 Scope

The assignment includes worldwide rights for the full duration of protection.

### 4.3 Future IP

The assignment applies to existing and future Intellectual Property created within the scope of engagement.

## 5. Moral Rights

### 5.1 Waiver

To the extent permitted by law, the Assignor waives any moral rights in the assigned Intellectual Property.

### 5.2 Non-Assertion

Where waiver is not legally possible, the Assignor agrees not to assert such rights against the Company.

## 6. Further Assurances

### 6.1 Cooperation

The Assignor shall execute any documents and take any actions necessary to perfect or register the Company’s ownership.

### 6.2 Survival

This obligation survives termination of employment or engagement.

## 7. Consideration

The assignment is made:
- as part of the Assignor’s employment or engagement, or
- in consideration of **[Number] [Currency]**

Unless otherwise required by law, no additional compensation shall be payable.

## 8. Pre-Existing Intellectual Property

### 8.1 Excluded IP

Intellectual Property developed prior to the Effective Date remains the property of the Assignor unless listed in Schedule 1 and expressly assigned.

### 8.2 License

If pre-existing Intellectual Property is incorporated into assigned work, the Assignor grants the Company a perpetual, royalty-free, worldwide license to use such Intellectual Property.

## 9. Representations and Warranties

The Assignor represents that:
- the Assignor is the sole creator and owner of the assigned Intellectual Property
- the assigned Intellectual Property does not infringe third-party rights
- the Assignor has full authority to enter into this Agreement

## 10. Indemnification

The Assignor shall indemnify the Company against losses arising from breach of the representations in Section 9.

## 11. Confidentiality

The Assignor shall keep confidential all proprietary information relating to the assigned Intellectual Property.

## 12. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[Location]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 13. Severability

If any provision is found invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.

## 14. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the assignment of Intellectual Property. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 15. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
