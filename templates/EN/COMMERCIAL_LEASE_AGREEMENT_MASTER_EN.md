# Commercial Lease Agreement

This Commercial Lease Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "Effective Date").

**Landlord**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF LANDLORD]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY/INDIVIDUAL]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**

**Tenant**
- Full legal name: **[FULL LEGAL NAME OF TENANT]**
- Entity type: **[COMPANY/INDIVIDUAL]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**

The Landlord and the Tenant are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Premises

### 2.1 Premises

The Landlord leases to the Tenant the premises located at **[FULL ADDRESS + DESCRIPTION]** (the "Premises").

### 2.2 Included Areas

The Premises include:
- **[Office space / storage / parking spaces / etc.]**

Further description is set out in Schedule 1.

## 3. Use of Premises

### 3.1 Permitted Use

The Premises shall be used solely for **[Describe permitted business activity]**.

### 3.2 Legal Compliance

The Tenant shall not use the Premises for unlawful purposes or in violation of zoning or regulatory requirements.

## 4. Term

### 4.1 Commencement

The lease commences on **[START DATE]**.

### 4.2 Fixed Term

The lease runs for a fixed term of **[X years]**, expiring on **[END DATE]**, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Agreement.

### 4.3 Continuation

If no notice of termination is given, the lease may continue on a rolling basis subject to applicable law.

## 5. Rent

### 5.1 Rent Amount

The annual rent is **[AMOUNT]** **[CURRENCY]**, payable in **[MONTHLY/QUARTERLY]** installments.

### 5.2 Due Date

Rent shall be paid in advance no later than **[DATE]** each payment period.

### 5.3 Payment Details

Payment shall be made to:
- Bank: **[Bank Name]**
- IBAN: **[IBAN]**

## 6. Rent Adjustment

### 6.1 Indexation

Rent shall be adjusted annually based on **[Net Price Index (NPI) / other agreed index]**.

### 6.2 No Decrease

Rent shall not decrease unless otherwise agreed.

## 7. Operating Costs

### 7.1 Reimbursable Costs

The Tenant shall reimburse the Landlord for:
- utilities
- property taxes
- maintenance of common areas
- other shared costs

### 7.2 Details

Details are set out in Schedule 2.

## 8. Security Deposit

### 8.1 Deposit

The Tenant shall pay a security deposit of **[AMOUNT]** **[CURRENCY]**, equivalent to **[X months’]** rent.

### 8.2 Use

The deposit may be used to cover unpaid rent or damages.

## 9. Maintenance and Repairs

### 9.1 Tenant Maintenance

The Tenant is responsible for internal maintenance of the Premises.

### 9.2 Landlord Maintenance

The Landlord is responsible for structural maintenance unless otherwise agreed.

### 9.3 Defects

The Tenant shall notify the Landlord promptly of defects requiring structural repair.

## 10. Alterations

### 10.1 Structural Alterations

The Tenant may not make structural alterations without prior written consent.

### 10.2 Ownership of Improvements

Improvements shall remain the property of the Landlord unless otherwise agreed.

## 11. Insurance

### 11.1 Tenant Insurance

The Tenant shall maintain adequate insurance coverage for its business operations.

### 11.2 Building Insurance

The Landlord shall maintain building insurance.

## 12. Subleasing and Assignment

### 12.1 Consent

The Tenant may not assign or sublease the Premises without prior written consent.

### 12.2 Reasonableness

Consent shall not be unreasonably withheld unless otherwise permitted by law.

## 13. Default

### 13.1 Events of Default

The Tenant is in default if:
- rent is overdue by more than **[X days]**
- material breach occurs

### 13.2 Remedies

In case of default, the Landlord may:
- demand immediate payment
- terminate the lease
- claim damages

## 14. Termination

### 14.1 Termination Rights

The lease may be terminated:
- by mutual agreement
- for breach
- in accordance with statutory notice requirements

### 14.2 Return of Premises

Upon termination, the Tenant shall vacate the Premises and return them in good condition, subject to normal wear and tear.

## 15. Force Majeure

Neither Party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable control.

## 16. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark. Any dispute shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[CITY, DENMARK]**, unless mandatory tenancy law provides otherwise.

## 17. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding the lease of the Premises. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 18. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
