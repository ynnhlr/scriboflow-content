## 2. Services

### 2.1 Services

The Provider shall deliver the services described in Schedule 1 (the "Services").

### 2.2 Standard of Performance

The Services shall be performed with reasonable skill, care, and professional diligence.

### 2.3 Subcontractors

The Provider may use subcontractors, provided the Provider remains responsible for performance.

## 3. Term

### 3.1 Commencement

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date.

### 3.2 Duration

The Agreement shall continue:
- until completion of the Services, or
- for an initial term of **[Number] months/years**, automatically renewing unless terminated in accordance with Section 11

## 4. Fees and Payment

### 4.1 Compensation

The Customer shall pay the Provider **[Number] [Currency]** or **[Number] [Currency]**, as set out in Schedule 1.

### 4.2 Invoicing

Invoices shall be issued **[Payment frequency]**.

### 4.3 Payment Terms

Invoices are payable within **[Number] days** from receipt.

### 4.4 Late Payment

Late payments may accrue statutory interest and reasonable recovery costs.

### 4.5 Suspension Rights

The Provider may suspend Services if payment is overdue after written notice.

## 5. Change Management

### 5.1 Changes

Any modification to the scope of Services must be agreed in writing.

### 5.2 Additional Work

Additional work outside the agreed scope may result in adjusted fees and timelines.

## 6. Customer Obligations

### 6.1 Cooperation

The Customer shall provide timely access to information, personnel, and systems necessary for performance.

### 6.2 Delays

Delays caused by the Customer may result in adjusted delivery schedules and additional costs.

## 7. Intellectual Property

### 7.1 Ownership of Deliverables

Unless otherwise agreed, ownership of deliverables transfers to the Customer upon full payment.

### 7.2 Pre-Existing Materials

Each Party retains ownership of its pre-existing materials.

### 7.3 License (If Applicable)

If ownership is not transferred, the Customer is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use deliverables for agreed purposes.

## 8. Confidentiality

### 8.1 Confidential Information

Each Party shall keep confidential information received from the other Party strictly confidential.

### 8.2 Survival

This obligation survives termination of the Agreement.

## 9. Warranties

### 9.1 Provider Warranties

The Provider warrants that the Services shall be performed professionally and in accordance with industry standards.

### 9.2 Disclaimer

Except as expressly stated, the Services are provided "as is".

## 10. Limitation of Liability

### 10.1 Indirect Damages

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages, including loss of profit.

### 10.2 Liability Cap

The Provider’s total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement during the preceding **[Number] months**, except in cases of fraud, gross negligence, or wilful misconduct.

## 11. Termination

### 11.1 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **[Number] days/months** written notice.

### 11.2 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate immediately in case of material breach not remedied within **[Number] days** after written notice.

### 11.3 Effects of Termination

Upon termination:
- all outstanding invoices become immediately due
- the Provider shall deliver completed work up to the termination date

## 12. Force Majeure

Neither Party shall be liable for failure to perform obligations due to events beyond reasonable control, including natural disasters, war, or government actions.

## 13. Data Protection

If the Provider processes personal data on behalf of the Customer, the Parties shall enter into a separate Data Processing Agreement.

## 14. Assignment

Neither Party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent, except in connection with a merger or business transfer.

## 15. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[Location]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 16. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes prior agreements relating to the Services. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 17. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
