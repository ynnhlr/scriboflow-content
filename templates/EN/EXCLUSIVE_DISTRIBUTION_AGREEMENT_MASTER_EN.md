This Exclusive Distribution Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "Effective Date").

- Entity type: **[COMPANY TYPE]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR No.: **[REGISTRATION NUMBER]**

The Supplier and the Distributor are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Appointment

### 2.1 Exclusivity

The Supplier appoints the Distributor as its exclusive distributor for the Products described in Schedule 1 within the Territory defined in Section 3.

### 2.2 Supplier Restrictions

During the term of this Agreement, the Supplier shall not appoint other distributors within the Territory nor actively sell directly into the Territory, subject to applicable competition law.

### 2.3 Distributor Undertaking

The Distributor accepts the appointment and undertakes to actively promote and sell the Products.

## 3. Territory

### 3.1 Territory

The Territory covered by this Agreement is **[DEFINE GEOGRAPHICAL AREA]** (the "Territory").

### 3.2 Outside Territory

The Distributor shall not actively sell outside the Territory without written consent.

### 3.3 Passive Sales

Passive sales into the Territory by third parties shall be governed by applicable competition law.

## 4. Products

### 4.1 Product List

The Products covered by this Agreement are listed in Schedule 1.

### 4.2 Product Updates

The Supplier may update or modify the Product range upon reasonable notice.

## 5. Performance Obligations

### 5.1 Distributor Obligations

The Distributor shall:
- maintain adequate inventory
- employ qualified sales personnel
- comply with brand and marketing guidelines
- meet minimum purchase targets set out in Schedule 2

### 5.2 Failure to Meet Targets

Failure to meet minimum targets may result in:
- conversion to non-exclusive status, or
- termination of this Agreement

## 6. Orders and Acceptance

### 6.1 Orders

All orders placed by the Distributor are subject to written acceptance by the Supplier.

### 6.2 Sales Contracts

Each accepted order forms a separate sales contract governed by this Agreement and the Supplier’s General Terms and Conditions of Sale.

## 7. Pricing and Payment

### 7.1 Prices

The Distributor shall purchase Products at the prices set out in Schedule 3.

### 7.2 Price Changes

The Supplier may adjust prices upon **[X days’]** written notice.

### 7.3 Payment Terms

Payment terms are **[X days]** from invoice date.

### 7.4 Late Payment

Late payment entitles the Supplier to suspend deliveries.

## 8. Intellectual Property

### 8.1 Ownership

All trademarks and intellectual property rights remain the property of the Supplier.

### 8.2 License

The Distributor is granted a limited, non-transferable license to use such rights solely for promoting and selling the Products.

### 8.3 Post-Termination

Upon termination, all use of intellectual property shall cease immediately.

## 9. Compliance with Law

### 9.1 Compliance

The Distributor shall comply with all applicable laws within the Territory.

### 9.2 Competition Law

The Parties shall comply with applicable competition and antitrust laws.

## 10. Warranties and Liability

### 10.1 Product Compliance

The Supplier warrants that Products comply with applicable laws at the time of delivery.

### 10.2 Liability Terms

Liability shall be governed by the Supplier’s General Terms and Conditions of Sale.

### 10.3 Indirect Damages

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages.

## 11. Term

### 11.1 Initial Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of **[X years]**.

### 11.2 Renewal

Thereafter, it shall automatically renew unless terminated with **[X months’]** written notice.

## 12. Termination

### 12.1 Immediate Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately in case of:
- material breach not remedied within **[X days]**
- insolvency
- repeated failure to meet performance obligations

### 12.2 Effects

Upon termination:
- outstanding payments become immediately due
- the Distributor shall cease representing itself as exclusive distributor
- remaining inventory arrangements shall be agreed in writing

## 13. Goodwill Compensation

Unless required by mandatory law, the Distributor shall not be entitled to compensation for goodwill or loss of business upon termination.

## 14. Confidentiality

Each Party shall treat confidential information received from the other Party as confidential.

## 15. Force Majeure

Neither Party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable control.

## 16. Assignment

Neither Party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent, except in connection with a merger or business transfer.

## 17. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[CITY, DENMARK]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 18. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the exclusive distribution of the Products. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 19. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
