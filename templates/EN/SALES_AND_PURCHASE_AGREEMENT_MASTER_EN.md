This Sales and Purchase Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[DATE]** (the "Effective Date").

## 1. Parties

**Seller**
- Full legal name: **[COMPANY NAME]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[CVR]**

**Buyer**
- Full legal name: **[COMPANY NAME]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[CVR]**

The Seller and the Buyer are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Sale of Assets

### 2.1 Sale

The Seller agrees to sell and transfer to the Buyer, and the Buyer agrees to purchase, the assets described in Schedule 1 (the "Assets").

### 2.2 Excluded Assets

The sale includes only the Assets expressly listed. No other assets, liabilities, or obligations are transferred unless explicitly stated.

## 3. Purchase Price

### 3.1 Price

The total purchase price for the Assets is **[NUMBER] [CURRENCY]** (the "Purchase Price").

### 3.2 Payment

Payment shall be made:
- in full on the Closing Date, or
- in accordance with the payment schedule set out in Schedule 2

### 3.3 VAT

The Purchase Price is exclusive of VAT unless otherwise specified.

## 4. Closing

### 4.1 Closing Date

Closing shall take place on **[DATE]** (the "Closing Date").

### 4.2 Closing Deliveries

On the Closing Date:
- the Seller shall transfer ownership of the Assets
- the Buyer shall pay the Purchase Price
- the Parties shall execute any additional transfer documents required

## 5. Transfer of Risk and Title

### 5.1 Risk

Risk of loss passes to the Buyer upon delivery of the Assets.

### 5.2 Title

Legal title transfers upon full payment of the Purchase Price unless otherwise agreed.

## 6. Representations and Warranties

### 6.1 Seller Representations

The Seller represents and warrants that:
- it has full authority to sell the Assets
- the Assets are free from liens and encumbrances unless disclosed in Schedule 1
- to its knowledge, the Assets do not infringe third-party rights

### 6.2 Buyer Representations

The Buyer represents and warrants that:
- it has authority to enter into this Agreement
- funds are available to pay the Purchase Price

## 7. No Assumption of Liabilities

The Buyer does not assume any liabilities of the Seller unless explicitly stated in this Agreement.

## 8. Indemnification

### 8.1 Seller Indemnity

The Seller shall indemnify the Buyer against losses arising from breach of representations and warranties.

### 8.2 Buyer Indemnity

The Buyer shall indemnify the Seller for losses arising from misuse of the Assets after Closing.

### 8.3 Claims Period

Claims must be made within **[NUMBER] months** after Closing unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 9. Limitation of Liability

### 9.1 Indirect Damages

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages.

### 9.2 Liability Cap

The Seller’s total liability shall not exceed the Purchase Price, except in cases of fraud or wilful misconduct.

## 10. Confidentiality

The Parties shall keep the terms of this Agreement confidential unless disclosure is required by law.

## 11. Taxes and Costs

Unless otherwise agreed:
- each Party bears its own legal and advisory costs
- transfer taxes or registration fees shall be borne by **[PARTY REFERENCE]**

## 12. Termination

### 12.1 Termination Before Closing

This Agreement may be terminated prior to Closing by mutual written agreement.

### 12.2 Effects

If terminated before Closing, neither Party shall have further obligations except for confidentiality.

## 13. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[LOCATION]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 14. Severability

If any provision is found invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.

## 15. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the sale of the Assets and supersedes prior negotiations or agreements. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 16. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
