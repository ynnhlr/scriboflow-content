This Employee Separation Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on **[DATE]** (the "Effective Date").

## 1. Parties

**Employer**
- Full legal name: **[COMPANY NAME]**
- Registered address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CVR/ID No.: **[CVR]**

**Employee**
- Full legal name: **[COMPANY NAME]**
- Address: **[ADDRESS]**
- CPR/ID No.: **[CVR]**

The Employer and the Employee are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Background

### 2.1 Employment Start

The Employee has been employed by the Employer since **[DATE]**.

### 2.2 Separation

The Parties have agreed to terminate the employment relationship under the terms set out in this Agreement.

## 3. Termination of Employment

### 3.1 Termination Date

The employment shall terminate on **[DATE]** (the "Termination Date").

### 3.2 Garden Leave

The Employee shall remain employed until the Termination Date unless placed on garden leave.

### 3.3 No Further Relationship

After the Termination Date, no further employment relationship shall exist between the Parties.

## 4. Final Compensation

### 4.1 Payments

The Employer shall pay:
- salary up to the Termination Date
- accrued but unused holiday pay in accordance with applicable law
- any outstanding expenses properly documented
- any additional severance compensation agreed in Section 4.2

### 4.2 Severance Payment (If Applicable)

The Employer shall pay the Employee a severance amount of **[NUMBER] [CURRENCY]**, subject to statutory deductions.

### 4.3 Tax Compliance

All payments shall be made in accordance with applicable tax regulations.

## 5. Release of Claims

### 5.1 Release

In consideration of the payments described above, the Employee irrevocably releases the Employer from any and all claims arising out of the employment relationship, whether known or unknown, up to the Effective Date.

### 5.2 Included Claims

This includes, but is not limited to, claims relating to:
- salary or compensation
- bonuses or commissions
- wrongful termination
- discrimination or harassment
- employment-related benefits

### 5.3 Non-Waivable Claims

This release does not waive claims that cannot be waived under mandatory law.

## 6. Confidentiality

### 6.1 Confidential Information

The Employee shall maintain confidentiality regarding:
- the terms of this Agreement
- confidential business information
- trade secrets and proprietary information

### 6.2 Survival

This obligation continues indefinitely.

## 7. Return of Company Property

The Employee confirms that all Company property, including equipment, documents, data, and access credentials, has been returned or shall be returned no later than the Termination Date.

## 8. Non-Competition and Non-Solicitation

Any existing non-compete or non-solicitation obligations remain in force in accordance with applicable law and prior agreements. If waived or modified, such modification must be expressly stated here: **[SPECIFY IF APPLICABLE]**.

## 9. Non-Disparagement (Optional)

The Parties agree not to make false or defamatory statements about each other following termination.

## 10. Admission of Liability

This Agreement does not constitute an admission of liability by either Party.

## 11. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[LOCATION]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 12. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the termination of employment. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 13. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original.
