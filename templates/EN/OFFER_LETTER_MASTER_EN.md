The anticipated start date is **[DATE]**, subject to fulfillment of the conditions set out in Section 8.

## 5. Working Hours

The normal working hours are **[NUMBER]** hours per week, subject to operational requirements and applicable law.

## 6. Holiday and Leave

The Candidate shall be entitled to holiday and statutory leave in accordance with applicable legislation.

## 7. Probation Period (If Applicable)

The employment shall be subject to a probation period of **[NUMBER] months**, during which termination may occur with shorter notice, in accordance with applicable law.

## 8. Conditions of Employment

This Offer is conditional upon:
- signing of a formal Employment Agreement
- verification of identity and right to work
- receipt of satisfactory references (if applicable)
- any other lawful background checks (if applicable)

If these conditions are not satisfied, the Offer may be withdrawn.

## 9. Non-Binding Nature

This Offer does not constitute a full employment contract. The employment relationship shall be governed by a separate written Employment Agreement to be executed by both Parties.

## 10. Confidentiality

The Candidate shall treat this Offer and any related discussions as confidential.

## 11. Governing Law

This Offer shall be governed by the laws of Denmark.

To accept this Offer, please confirm acceptance in writing no later than **[DATE]**. Failure to accept by this date may result in withdrawal of the Offer.
