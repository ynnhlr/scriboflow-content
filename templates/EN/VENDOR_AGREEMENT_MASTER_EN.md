The Customer and the Vendor are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 2. Scope of Supply

### 2.1 Deliverables

The Vendor shall supply the goods and/or services described in Schedule 1 (the "Deliverables").

### 2.2 Specifications

The Deliverables shall meet agreed specifications, quality standards, and applicable laws.

### 2.3 Changes

No changes to the Deliverables shall be made without prior written approval.

## 3. Orders

### 3.1 Purchase Orders

The Customer may issue purchase orders referencing this Agreement.

### 3.2 Acceptance

Each accepted purchase order forms part of this Agreement.

### 3.3 Order of Precedence

In case of conflict, this Agreement shall prevail over any Vendor terms.

## 4. Pricing and Payment

### 4.1 Pricing

Prices are specified in Schedule 2.

### 4.2 Fixed Prices

Prices are fixed unless otherwise agreed in writing.

### 4.3 Payment Terms

Invoices shall be payable within **[NUMBER] days** from receipt, provided Deliverables comply with this Agreement.

### 4.4 Disputed Amounts

The Customer may withhold payment for disputed amounts.

## 5. Delivery

### 5.1 Delivery Terms

Delivery shall take place at **[LOCATION] [INCOTERM]**.

### 5.2 Time of the Essence

Time is of the essence unless otherwise agreed.

### 5.3 Delay Notice

The Vendor shall notify the Customer immediately of any anticipated delay.

### 5.4 Cancellation

The Customer may cancel orders in case of material delay.

## 6. Inspection and Acceptance

### 6.1 Inspection

The Customer may inspect Deliverables within a reasonable period after delivery.

### 6.2 Rejection

Non-conforming Deliverables may be rejected.

### 6.3 Remedies

The Vendor shall, at its own cost, repair, replace, or refund non-conforming Deliverables.

## 7. Warranties

### 7.1 Warranty Scope

The Vendor warrants that Deliverables:
- conform to agreed specifications
- are free from defects
- comply with applicable laws
- do not infringe third-party intellectual property rights

### 7.2 Warranty Period

The warranty period shall be **[NUMBER] months** from delivery unless otherwise agreed.

## 8. Compliance and Ethics

### 8.1 Legal Compliance

The Vendor shall comply with all applicable laws, including labor, tax, environmental, and anti-corruption regulations.

### 8.2 Documentation

Upon reasonable request, the Vendor shall provide documentation demonstrating compliance.

## 9. Confidentiality

### 9.1 Confidential Information

The Vendor shall keep confidential all business and technical information received from the Customer.

### 9.2 Survival

Confidentiality obligations survive termination.

## 10. Intellectual Property

### 10.1 Work Product

Any work product specifically developed for the Customer shall belong to the Customer upon full payment.

### 10.2 Assignment

The Vendor assigns all transferable intellectual property rights in such work product to the Customer.

### 10.3 Pre-Existing IP

Pre-existing intellectual property remains the property of the respective Party.

## 11. Indemnification

The Vendor shall indemnify the Customer against losses arising from:
- defective Deliverables
- breach of this Agreement
- third-party intellectual property claims

## 12. Limitation of Liability

### 12.1 Indirect Damages

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages.

### 12.2 Liability Cap

The Vendor’s total liability shall not exceed **[NUMBER] amount or contract value**, except in cases of gross negligence, wilful misconduct, or product liability.

## 13. Insurance

The Vendor shall maintain adequate insurance coverage for:
- product liability
- professional liability (if applicable)
- general commercial liability

Proof of insurance shall be provided upon request.

## 14. Term and Termination

### 14.1 Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until terminated.

### 14.2 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate with **[NUMBER] days/months** written notice.

### 14.3 Termination for Cause

The Customer may terminate immediately in case of:
- material breach
- repeated quality issues
- insolvency

## 15. Force Majeure

Neither Party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable control.

## 16. Assignment

The Vendor may not assign this Agreement without prior written consent. The Customer may assign this Agreement in connection with a business transfer.

## 17. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, excluding conflict-of-law rules. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[LOCATION]**, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

## 18. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the Deliverables. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 19. Execution

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
